About LAKE TINY

Located in the east side of Coimbra, in center Potugal, the LAKE TINY is your perfect spot to escape and connect with nature. Here you have a micro village of tiny houses to enjoy a romantic weekend, have fun with your family and friends, or recharge bateries by yourself.
All the 5 Tiny Houses are equally beautifully decorated and fully equiped with all the essentials so you don't have to bring much.
You're allways greeted with a delicious breakfast composed of a variety of local products, and on the first night that's on us! For the rest of your stay you can still have it upon request.
The hot summer days invite you for a dip in the beautifull green lake, 20 meter away from our Tiny Houses.
And if the winter weather stops you from that epic sweem, each Tiny has an amazing private tub where you can enjoy a hot bath outside.
